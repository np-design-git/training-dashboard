**9. Progression Logic**

**9.1 Standard Strength and Hypertrophy**

-   Start at 3 x 8 reps at current working weight

-   Progress week over week: 8 to 10 to 12 (same weight throughout)

-   Once 12 reps achieved cleanly across all sets: increase weight,
    reset to 8

-   Weight increase: +2.5 kg for loads under 30 kg \| +5% for loads 30
    kg and above

-   NEVER increase weight AND reps in the same session

-   Form quality is the ceiling - not schedule or motivation
